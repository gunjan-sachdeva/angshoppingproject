Frequently Asked Questions

What is the return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and with all original tags and packaging.

How can I track my order?

You will receive an email with a tracking number once your order has been shipped. You can also check your order status on our website by logging in to your account.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping. Please note that there may be additional fees for customs and duties.

Can I cancel or change my order?

We process orders quickly, but you can try contacting us to see if changes can be made before your order ships.

How do I contact customer service?

You can email us at customerservice@vestirium.com or call us at 1-800-555-5555. Our customer service hours are Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm EST.
